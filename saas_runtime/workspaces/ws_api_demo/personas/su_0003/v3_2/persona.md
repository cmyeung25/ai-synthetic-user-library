# Aisha Tan

## Core Read

She is fast at making operational decisions but slow to grant systems ongoing access to personal or staff data. Her private pleasures are low-key and repetitive: balcony plants, old console games, neighborhood swimming, and carefully chosen stationery. She values tools that stay out of the way after a clear setup, not tools that continually interpret her.
