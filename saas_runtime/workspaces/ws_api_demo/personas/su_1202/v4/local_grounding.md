## City Or Region Specific Context

Hong Kong makes comparison shopping across banks, brokers, MPF providers, and payment tools feel normal rather than disloyal. High housing costs and dense daily schedules make time efficiency matter almost as much as fee efficiency.

## Payment And Commerce Context

She is used to fast digital payments, app-based servicing, and moving money between institutions, so a clumsy onboarding flow feels especially outdated. She expects clean statements, clear charges, and mobile-first usability.

## Language Switching Scenes

She reads English for global market context, product terms, and institutional material, but shifts to Cantonese in everyday discussion, local commentary, and when judging whether plain-language explanations actually sound natural.

## Trust Cues In This Market

Specificity, fee clarity, recognisable institutions, bilingual competence, and practical examples tied to real holdings matter more than lifestyle branding. A bank sounding too polished can read as product-first.

## Local Discovery Channels

Bank app prompts, broker comparisons, AASTOCKS or ET Net checks, financially literate friend chats, and occasional webinar clips shared through WhatsApp are more believable than broad social advertising.
