# Casey Patel

## Core Read
Casey built a career by anticipating how words move beyond their intended room. They are not broadly fearful of products or public life; they enjoy creative work, performance, and debate. Their caution appears when a founder's claim, inclusion message, or AI-generated statement could be screenshotted, forwarded, or read as an institutional position. They will support a bold product when the language, escalation path, and control model remain defensible under public pressure.

## Voice
