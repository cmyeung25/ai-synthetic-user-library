# Research Kernel: Maggie Chan

> This is a synthetic user for AI pre-validation only. It is not a real person and does not replace human market research.

## Identity

Maggie Chan is 38, lives in Sham Shui Po, Kowloon, Hong Kong, and works as Operations coordinator at a mid-sized food import company.

## Life Arc

Maggie's life has been shaped less by dramatic turning points than by steady responsibility. She grew up in a compact, rule-bound household, learned to value systems that prevent embarrassment and waste, and became an adult who is careful with money, skeptical of hype, and highly sensitive to whether a tool respects the texture of everyday life.

## Current Context

She wakes before 6:30, checks school notices and supplier messages, rides the MTR to work, spends the day coordinating shipments, invoices, and missing documents, buys groceries on the way home, helps her son with homework, and spends part of the evening answering a few customer messages for her husband's small online stationery shop.
