# Sensitive Scenarios: Maggie Chan

> This is a synthetic user for AI pre-validation only. It is not a real person and does not replace human market research.

## Identity Disclosure

comfort level: Conditional; reaction: She shares only what seems necessary for function and prefers optional rather than mandatory disclosure.; risk signal: Forms that ask for personal identity details without a clear operational reason.; adaptive need: Explain purpose and let her skip or revise later.

## Privacy And Data

comfort level: Low if vague, moderate if specific; reaction: She becomes more analytical and slows down the purchase decision when data handling is unclear.; risk signal: Broad permissions, unclear retention, or account linking before trust is built.; adaptive need: Plain-language privacy summary and bounded permissions.

## Political Or Public Expression

comfort level: Low; reaction: She avoids products that could unexpectedly make her views visible or socially legible in public ways.; risk signal: Social posting defaults, public communities tied to sensitive topics, or identity-signaling prompts.; adaptive need: Strong privacy defaults and separation between use and public expression.

## Fairness And Inclusion

comfort level: Moderate; reaction: She responds positively to quiet competence and respectful flexibility, not slogan-heavy framing.; risk signal: Family, language, or gender assumptions embedded in core flows.; adaptive need: Inclusive defaults that do not ask her to perform identity.

## Family Or Household Assumptions

comfort level: Moderate-low for narrow assumptions; reaction: She notices immediately if a product assumes one caretaker, one language, or one style of family authority.; risk signal: Rigid household templates and moralized parenting language.; adaptive need: Configurable family roles and neutral wording.

## Workplace Visibility

comfort level: Guarded; reaction: She does not want a new tool to make her look careless, disorganized, or naive at work.; risk signal: Products that generate obvious errors or require evangelizing before proving themselves.; adaptive need: Pilot mode, exportability, and dependable outputs.

## Financial Vulnerability

comfort level: Guarded but discussable in practical terms; reaction: She will talk about affordability as budget discipline, not as personal weakness.; risk signal: Shame-based upsells or assumptions that affordability concerns are minor.; adaptive need: Respectful low-commitment entry points.

## Health Or Wellbeing Sensitivity

comfort level: Moderate if private and useful; reaction: She is open to support but avoids products that pathologize ordinary stress.; risk signal: Diagnostic tone, judgmental scoring, or aggressive reminders.; adaptive need: Private, low-pressure framing with clear limits.
