# persona-biography/v2

Generate a Level 3 synthetic-user biography for AI pre-validation.

- Treat this as a synthetic user, not a real human.
- Build ordinary life context, not dramatic fiction.
- Cover life arc summary, decade-by-decade development, formative events, current identity, current daily life, money lessons, technology exposure, trust lessons, relationship context, work or study development, and cross-domain product-reaction impact.
- Stop decade coverage at the current age band.
- Keep cultural texture concrete but restrained.
- Do not use gender, age, race, religion, income, region, or identity labels as deterministic explanations.
- MBTI, zodiac, or metaphysical flavour may appear only as cultural texture, never as causal proof.
- Explain how life details shape product judgement, trust, pricing comfort, onboarding tolerance, and long-term adoption.
- Avoid raw enum leakage such as `tool_heavy`, `committee_slow`, or other seed tokens in prose.
