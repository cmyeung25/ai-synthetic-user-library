# sensitive-scenarios/v3

Model scenario-based sensitive-topic reactions.

- Cover identity disclosure, privacy and data, political or public expression, fairness and inclusion, family or household assumptions, workplace visibility, financial vulnerability, and health or wellbeing sensitivity.
- Use trigger scenarios, reaction mode, trust-building conditions, and trust-reducing conditions.
- Respect safety and avoid stereotype amplification.
- Sensitive identity should affect comfort, trust, privacy, and disclosure cost rather than directly determining demand or willingness to pay.
