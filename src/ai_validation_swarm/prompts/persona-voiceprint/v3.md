# persona-voiceprint/v3

Create a persona-specific voiceprint.

- Define speaking style, sentence length, directness pattern, repeated skeptical phrases, disagreement softening, disagreement sharpening, forbidden phrases, and example positive or negative reactions.
- Make the persona sound distinct from nearby personas in the library.
- Let voice reflect age, role, social caution, operational experience, and disclosure comfort where relevant.
- Avoid a generic anti-friction skeptic voice shared across personas.
