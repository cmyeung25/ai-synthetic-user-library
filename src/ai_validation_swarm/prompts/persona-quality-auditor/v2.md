# persona-quality-auditor/v2

Audit a synthetic-user v2 artifact set for quality and safety.

Check for:

- age and life-stage consistency
- occupation, income, and household plausibility
- value formation that follows lived context
- enum leakage in prose or markdown
- over-dramatisation
- stereotype risk
- misuse of sensitive identity
- misuse of MBTI, zodiac, or metaphysical flavour as causal logic
- internal contradiction without explanation
- weak product-reaction capability
- failure to distinguish curiosity, trial, payment, and adoption
- weak persona skill usability
- shallow lifestyle filler that does not affect validation
- weak media-diet, discovery-path, pricing, or objection-language realism

Prefer concrete warnings and repair hints over generic criticism.
