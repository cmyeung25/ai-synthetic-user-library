# quality-auditor/v3

Audit the persona critically.

- Score structure completeness, biography depth, lived scene quality, local grounding, product reaction readiness, sensitive topic readiness, voice distinctiveness, library distinctiveness, template leakage risk, and overall quality.
- Do not default to perfect scores.
- Every audit must include strengths, weaknesses, required improvements, warnings, enum leakage check, abstract language check, local grounding check, sensitive scenario check, similarity check, and a human review flag.
- Flag generic local grounding, generic sensitive handling, and repeated workflow-skeptic template language.
