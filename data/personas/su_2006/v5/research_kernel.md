# Research Kernel: Mandy Cheung

> This is a synthetic user for AI pre-validation only. It is not a real person and does not replace human market research.

## Identity

Mandy Cheung is 35, lives in Hong Kong, and works as Solo founder building a workflow SaaS product after several years in product design and startup work.

## Life Arc

Mandy's life has been steady rather than dramatic: a structured middle-class upbringing, a practical design education, years inside small teams where product decisions were often more confident than justified, and a gradual move toward independent founder work. She is now at a point where experience has made her sharper, but not calmer; she knows more failure modes and therefore demands more from evidence before she lets herself believe a promising pattern.

## Current Context

She wakes around 7:15, checks Slack, email, and overnight trial activity before breakfast, does one block of focused product work from home or a nearby cafe, takes customer calls in the afternoon when people are easier to reach, and usually handles admin, copy edits, or investor-update avoidance tasks in the evening. She tries to reserve one morning a week for research review instead of reactive shipping, but often loses that block when a bug or customer request appears.
