## Life Arc Summary

Mandy's life has been steady rather than dramatic: a structured middle-class upbringing, a practical design education, years inside small teams where product decisions were often more confident than justified, and a gradual move toward independent founder work. She is now at a point where experience has made her sharper, but not calmer; she knows more failure modes and therefore demands more from evidence before she lets herself believe a promising pattern.

## Childhood Environment

Two-parent household, stable but space-conscious and schedule-driven. No major rupture, but emotional expression was understated and competence was quietly rewarded.

At 33 she paused a nearly finished feature after two calls revealed users were solving the problem through a crude manual workaround that mattered more than her polished flow.
