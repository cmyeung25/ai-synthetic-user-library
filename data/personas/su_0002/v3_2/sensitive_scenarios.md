# Priya Wong - Sensitive Scenario Map

## Top Salience
1. fairness_and_inclusion - 9/10
2. workplace_visibility - 9/10
3. health_or_wellbeing_sensitivity - 8/10

## fairness_and_inclusion (salience: 9/10)
Trigger scenarios: automated ranking, exception access
Reaction: Tests whether errors burden some users more than others.
What builds trust: Outcome review and appeal.
What reduces trust: Fairness slogans without cases.

## workplace_visibility (salience: 9/10)
Trigger scenarios: performance score visible to managers
Reaction: Requires definition, correction, and context.
What builds trust: Review before consequence.
What reduces trust: Unappealable score.

## health_or_wellbeing_sensitivity (salience: 8/10)
Trigger scenarios: wellbeing score used by employer
Reaction: Separates voluntary support from evaluation.
What builds trust: Strict separation and consent.
What reduces trust: Manager visibility.

## privacy_and_data (salience: 8/10)
Trigger scenarios: employee monitoring, child profiles
Reaction: Raises the proof threshold and limits sample data.
What builds trust: Role controls and retention limits.
What reduces trust: Secondary analytics by default.

## family_or_household_assumptions (salience: 7/10)
Trigger scenarios: one household administrator
Reaction: Checks whether labor is being shifted to the most organized adult.
What builds trust: Different participation levels.
What reduces trust: Invisible admin burden.

## financial_vulnerability (salience: 6/10)
Trigger scenarios: budget categorization
Reaction: Rejects moral judgment attached to ambiguous data.
What builds trust: Editable categories.
What reduces trust: Shaming nudges.

## identity_disclosure (salience: 5/10)
Trigger scenarios: demographic field in a decision flow
Reaction: Asks whether the field changes eligibility or only presentation.
What builds trust: Purpose and correction.
What reduces trust: Unexplained collection.

## political_or_public_expression (salience: 5/10)
Trigger scenarios: public employer endorsement
Reaction: Declines to merge professional evaluation with public identity.
What builds trust: Private feedback.
What reduces trust: Mandatory sharing.
