# Casey Patel - Sensitive Scenario Map

## Top Salience
1. political_or_public_expression - 10/10
2. workplace_visibility - 10/10
3. fairness_and_inclusion - 9/10

## political_or_public_expression (salience: 10/10)
Trigger scenarios: institutional statement, employee advocacy
Reaction: Tests ownership, evidence, and unintended association.
What builds trust: Explicit voice and opt-in.
What reduces trust: Compelled public alignment.

## workplace_visibility (salience: 10/10)
Trigger scenarios: draft attributed as final position
Reaction: Requires version and approval state.
What builds trust: Clear draft markers.
What reduces trust: Ambiguous institutional authorship.

## fairness_and_inclusion (salience: 9/10)
Trigger scenarios: inclusive campaign with rigid form
Reaction: Checks operational experience against public claim.
What builds trust: Control matching language.
What reduces trust: Performative wording.

## identity_disclosure (salience: 9/10)
Trigger scenarios: public gender marker, campaign asking employee identity story
Reaction: Separates voluntary personal disclosure from institutional use.
What builds trust: Audience choice and revocable consent.
What reduces trust: Identity used as proof of brand values.

## privacy_and_data (salience: 8/10)
Trigger scenarios: AI training on drafts, public profile default
Reaction: Requests provenance and publication boundaries.
What builds trust: Clear retention and draft privacy.
What reduces trust: Secondary use or auto-sharing.

## health_or_wellbeing_sensitivity (salience: 7/10)
Trigger scenarios: lock-screen health language, employer wellbeing campaign
Reaction: Tests what another viewer would infer.
What builds trust: Neutral private wording.
What reduces trust: Diagnostic public copy.

## family_or_household_assumptions (salience: 6/10)
Trigger scenarios: partner equals shared identity
Reaction: Keeps payment and disclosure roles separate.
What builds trust: Independent profiles.
What reduces trust: Couple-level public defaults.

## financial_vulnerability (salience: 5/10)
Trigger scenarios: contract status exposed in pricing profile
Reaction: Rejects identity conclusions from payment timing.
What builds trust: Private flexible billing.
What reduces trust: Public affordability segment.
