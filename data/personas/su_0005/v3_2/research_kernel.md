## Childhood Foundations

Early emotional and authority pattern: Affectionate but verbally measured. Strong disagreement was allowed at home, while public composure and not humiliating another person were treated as social responsibilities. Casey learned that formal rules and public messages could have audiences beyond their stated recipient.
