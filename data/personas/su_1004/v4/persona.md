That makes her receptive to any product promising to reduce WhatsApp noise, payment awkwardness, and the familiar burden of being the reliable person who ends up booking for everyone. She is not looking for inspiration as much as containment: a way to move a small group from maybe to confirmed without exposing her full schedule, fronting too much money, or begging friends to install something complicated. If it creates even one extra layer of admin, she will abandon it quickly and explain exactly why.
