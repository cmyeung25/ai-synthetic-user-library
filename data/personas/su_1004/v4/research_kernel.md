# Research Kernel: Chloe Lau

> This is a synthetic user for AI pre-validation only. It is not a real person and does not replace human market research.

## Identity

Chloe Lau is 35, lives in Hong Kong urban districts, and works as operations manager at a professional training and events company.

## Life Arc

Chloe grew up in a stable but budget-conscious Hong Kong household where punctuality, keeping your word, and not creating trouble for others mattered. As an adult she became professionally skilled at coordination, which made her useful to friends but also vulnerable to becoming the unpaid organizer. She wants tools that return social planning to something light and enjoyable rather than another operations role.

## Current Context

She wakes around 7, checks work messages and MTR timing before leaving, spends most of the day juggling calendars, venue details, invoices, and follow-ups, and usually clears personal messages in bursts on the commute or after dinner. Weeknights are for low-effort meals, one or two chores, and deciding whether she has enough energy to say yes to weekend plans.

## Pricing Logic
