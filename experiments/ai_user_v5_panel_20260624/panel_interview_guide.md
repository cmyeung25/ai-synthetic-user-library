# AI User V5 Panel Interview Guide

Date: 2026-06-24

## Panel Objective

Use three newly generated V5 personas to test the concept of `AI user / synthetic user` as a serious product-research and behavior-simulation tool.

Primary question:

- Under what conditions would product-building professionals trust AI users enough to use them in real discovery, concept evaluation, or prototype-validation work?

Secondary questions:

- Where would they place this tool in an actual product workflow?
- What would make them treat it as useful exploration versus real evidence?
- What would make them reject it?

## Panelists

### 1. Mandy Cheung

- Persona ID: `su_2006`
- Role: solo founder with product design background
- Best panel function: founder-side evaluator of decision usefulness, speed, and evidence credibility
- Likely focus:
  - whether the tool catches blind spots she genuinely missed
  - whether it improves founder decision quality or just generates nice-looking output
  - whether it saves runway rather than creating evidence theatre

### 2. Janice Wong

- Persona ID: `su_2007`
- Role: product manager at a mid-stage software company
- Best panel function: PM-side evaluator of workflow fit, stakeholder defensibility, and adoption in real teams
- Likely focus:
  - whether the output can survive review by design, engineering, and management
  - whether the method is explainable enough to influence prioritization
  - whether the tool helps real roadmap decisions instead of just early curiosity

### 3. Maggie Leung Wai-ting

- Persona ID: `su_2008`
- Role: senior UX designer with interview and prototype-testing exposure
- Best panel function: UX-side evaluator of nuance retention, behavioral plausibility, and research integrity
- Likely focus:
  - whether the tool preserves contradictions and edge cases
  - whether it confuses articulate answers with believable behavior
  - whether it helps synthesis without flattening users into neat summaries

## Recommended Moderator Framing

Use this framing at the start:

> 今日唔係要你哋抽象咁講 AI 好唔好，而係想你哋當自己真係要喺工作入面用一個 `AI user / synthetic user` 工具，去幫手做 discovery、concept evaluation 同 prototype validation。請你哋盡量用真實工作流程去判斷：你會點用、唔會點用、邊啲位會信、邊啲位唔會信。

Important moderator rule:

- Do not ask whether AI users can "replace humans" too early.
- First anchor them in workflow, evidence type, and decision context.
- Push for concrete usage conditions, not abstract opinions.

## Suggested 30-Minute Flow

### Round 1: Current-state roadmap and prioritization

Ask all three:

1. 你而家實際上係點樣決定 roadmap？
2. 一個新功能由出現到入 roadmap，中間通常經過咩判斷？
3. 你而家決定 feature priority 最主要睇咩？

Follow-up probes:

- 係邊個提出功能？通常係 founder、PM、design、sales、客戶，定其他人？
- 你會睇邊幾類 signal？例如 user interview、support tickets、usage data、sales feedback、gut feeling、competitive pressure？
- 有冇一套固定方法，定通常係 case by case？
- 當 evidence 唔夠，但又要快決定時，你而家點做？
- 邊個人最終拍板？點樣處理唔同 function 之間意見唔同？

What to listen for:

- Mandy: founder intuition versus actual validation rhythm
- Janice: cross-functional decision process and stakeholder defensibility
- Maggie: how qualitative signals are currently translated into priority

### Round 2: Current pain in the existing process

Ask:

1. 你對而家 deciding roadmap / feature priority 呢套方法，最唔滿意係邊度？
2. 最常見做錯判斷嘅位係咩？
3. 有冇啲位你覺得而家係靠經驗、靠感覺、或者靠最有聲量嗰個人？

Follow-up probes:

- 你而家最缺乏嘅係速度、信心、用戶理解，定 alignment？
- 有冇啲情況係你明知 evidence 唔夠，但都要照排優先度？
- 邊類 feature 最容易被高估？邊類 feature 最容易被忽略？

### Round 3: Immediate framing of AI user

Ask all three:

1. 你第一次聽到 `AI user / synthetic user`，你直覺會當佢係咩？
2. 你會將佢理解成一個 research tool、decision tool、定 simulation tool？點解？
3. 如果你公司有人提議用呢樣嘢，你第一個 concern 會係咩？

What to listen for:

- Mandy: whether this addresses founder-side prioritization blind spots
- Janice: whether this fits the real decision stack instead of becoming another input source
- Maggie: whether this helps current synthesis gaps without flattening nuance

### Round 4: Workflow placement

Ask:

1. 如果要放入你現有 workflow，最合理係放喺邊一步？
2. 你會用佢做 discovery、concept screening、prototype critique，定其他位？
3. 邊一步你覺得佢可以加速，邊一步你覺得唔應該代替真人？

Follow-up probes:

- 你會喺做真人訪談之前用，定之後用？
- 你會當佢係 pre-filter，定 evidence source？
- 如果只准揀一個 use case，會係邊個？

### Round 5: Trust and evidence threshold

Ask:

1. 咩情況下你會開始相信呢個 `AI user` 唔只係識答，而係真係捕捉到接近真實人類行為？
2. 你需要睇到咩 proof 先會信？
3. 邊類 output 最容易呃到人，但其實未必可信？

Follow-up probes:

- 你會想睇 reasoning、source logic、persona construction，定 historical consistency？
- 如果 output 好流暢，但唔知點樣得出，你仲會唔會用？
- 你會用咩方式驗證佢唔係只係 mirror 你原本假設？

### Round 6: Replacement boundary

Ask:

1. 喺你工作入面，邊一部分你覺得 AI user 最有可能取代真人研究？
2. 邊一部分你覺得永遠唔應該直接取代？
3. 如果有人話佢可以「取代 user interview」，你會點 challenge 呢句說話？

Follow-up probes:

- 係唔係取代 recruitment 成本，而唔係取代 evidence？
- 係唔係可以取代 early exploration，但唔可以取代 final validation？
- 你最介意佢取代咗邊種 human signal？

### Round 7: Adoption decision

Close with:

1. 如果三個月內你真係要試一次，你會點樣 pilot？
2. 乜嘢結果會令你繼續用？
3. 乜嘢結果會令你覺得呢件事只係另一種包裝？

## Persona-Specific Prompts

### Best prompts for Mandy

- 如果你係 founder，呢樣嘢幫你慳到嘅係 research time，定 decision regret？
- 你會點分辨佢係 challenge 緊你，定只係順住你原本假設講？
- 如果你要用自己張卡畀錢，你會為咩而買？

### Best prompts for Janice

- 如果你要向 manager、designer、engineer 解釋點解要用，最難 defend 係邊部分？
- 呢個 output 可唔可以入 prioritization discussion？
- 你會點定義佢係 exploratory input 定 decision-grade evidence？

### Best prompts for Maggie

- 你點分辨一個 synthetic user 係「講得似」定「行為上似」？
- 呢個工具有冇 flatten 晒 contradictions 同 edge cases？
- 如果佢俾你好整齊嘅 summary，你最擔心 summary 洗走咗咩？

## What Good Signal Looks Like

- The panelists place the product in a specific workflow stage instead of discussing AI in general.
- They distinguish between:
  - interesting output
  - useful exploration
  - decision-grade evidence
- They name concrete trust thresholds such as:
  - need to see how outputs were formed
  - need side-by-side comparison with real interview or known user behavior
  - need proof that the tool surfaces disagreement rather than smoothing it away
- They explain the current roadmap and prioritization process clearly enough that you can see where the tool would or would not fit.
- They define a replacement boundary rather than giving a total yes or no.

## What Weak Signal Looks Like

- They answer only with broad AI opinions.
- They stay at abstract ethics or hype language without talking about workflow.
- They keep saying "depends" without naming what it depends on.
- The discussion skips current-state process and jumps straight to future-tool opinions.
- The discussion never separates:
  - simulation
  - research
  - validation
  - persuasion inside a team

## Recommended First Three Questions

If you want the shortest usable opening sequence, start with these:

1. 你而家實際上係點樣決定 roadmap，同埋每個 feature 的 priority？
2. 你對而家呢套做法最唔滿意係邊度？
3. 如果有人提議用 `AI user / synthetic user` 幫你哋做判斷，你第一反應會當佢係咩工具？
