Casey Patel is a 62-year-old non-binary communications manager in Kuching, living with a partner and one child. Casey is experienced, digitally capable, and careful rather than conservative: open to better systems, but only when they reduce actual weekly strain without creating new ambiguity. The main pressure is not technical ability; it is the accumulation of small coordination tasks spread across messages, notes, reminders, and budget scrutiny. Casey wants tools that make follow-up more reliable, preserve privacy, and hold up under peer and manager scrutiny. The fastest way to earn trust is to show a believable workflow, a short path to value, and proof that similar teams used it without adding mess.
